The Problem

We heard the young, scrappy musicians, comics, and filmmakers, independently working tirelessly within their bedroom to capture the perfect sound.

For most young content creators, it's impossible to justify big budgets when you're starting out. But a high quality is required to make it. This deficit means that many young content creators are unable to get the equipment they need to turn a passion into a career.

So, we know that creators are dissatisfied. But, there’s more to this. Let’s talk about why audio quality is so important. Research published in the Journal of the Audio Engineering Society found that low-quality audio made audiences perceive the quality of a Youtube video overall as significantly lower. It is no surprise either that videos with lower quality audio are likely to have poor retention rates. But here’s something even more striking: a recent study at the Australian National University found that people listening to recordings of scientists discussing their research believed them to be less intelligent if they were listening to a low-quality recording. Needless to say, audio quality matters.

There’s a conundrum here. Independent creators- the musicians recording from their garage, the Youtubers filming in their bedrooms, the podcast hosts taping with their phones- they deserve to be heard. They deserve to project their great ideas into the world, and not have poor audio quality hold them back from their fullest potential.

Our Solution

Enter Suono. We handle all of the above. With Suono, creators don’t have to choose between functionality, affordability, or style- instead they can focus on creating their highest quality work.

The Product

Benefits

Suono uses advanced sound-reduction technology to ensure that creators have a space suitable for their needs, allowing them to focus on developing their best, most high-quality work.

1) Affordability and Customer Experience

"$9 a panel"

Suono also offers a valuable customer experience that goes beyond just sound reduction. With a team committed to ensuring a quality product without breaking the bank, Suono provides a reasonably priced solution, at just $9 a panel.

2) Effectiveness and Quality

The most important benefit, is the effectiveness and quality of our product. To ensure this, we consulted with Professor Wayne Jones before diving into the prototyping phase. We learned that one thick panel is far less effective than a composite panel made up of one piece of material, an air gap, and then a second piece of material. This construction is often referred to as a double leaf effect.

Using this approach, we were able to engineer a solution using thick stone wool. We made sure to cut each section into different thicknesses, encasing a pocket of air. Because sound waves lose energy when traveling through different densities, we’re sure that this approach is going to decrease the amount of sound passing through the panel.

3) Strength in Bulk

Another benefit of the panels, when used together, is that they reduce the amount of sound reflected as it would bounce off of one another. To encourage this, we sell our panels in packs of 10 at a discounted rate!

3) Style

The final plus of Suono, and personally my favorite, is the style! Almost all soundproofing solutions on the market today are boring, monochrome, and honestly just ugly. Suono hopes to change this by offering a variety of stylish designs. Our customers are creatives, and we want to encourage this! Our goal is that they appreciate all aspects of the product, not just it’s functionality and affordability.
