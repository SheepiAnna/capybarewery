Our Story
Founding a Brewery

Capybrewery is a fictional brewery started by father/daughter duo Roman Shaposhnik and Anna Shaposhnik. My day loves Capybara memes and craft beer, so for his birthday one year I decided to make both come together in "Capybarewery": Capybara + Bar + Brewery. Thus, from the logo was born an entire brand, and now this website!
